Overview

Purpose

This site is to drive sales for our rafting guide company. We want to make it easy for customers to learn about different packages, book tours online, and get excited about their next guided rafting experience.

Audience

Who are our target customers? Parents and grandparents of older, established families with mostly grown up children, or other adult friends who can afford the guided trip, as well as a generous tip. What do they want? A vacation that will be filled with adventure, quality family time and bonding activities, and an escape from the noise. What do they expect to find on our website? Lots of information about rafting, basic gear lists, what they can expect on one of our guided tours, and the different packages we offer along with a way to purchase and schedule a guided tour.
